This Privacy Policy explains how the **Self Help App** ("App"), operated by **L.T.O consultants LTD** ("we", "us", "our"), collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the App. By using the App, you consent to this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

- Device information (OS, device type)  
- Usage data (pages viewed, time spent, session duration)  
- IP address

We do not collect precise geolocation.

### User-Provided Information
For features such as account creation and chatbot:  
- Email address  
- Password  
- Display name  
- User ID  
- Google Sign-In data (if used)  
- AI Chatbot conversation history (recent messages kept for session context)  

Chat history is permanently deleted upon account deletion via the "Delete Account" button in Settings.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain App features (chatbot context, exercises)  
- To improve the App (anonymized & aggregated data only)  
- For important notices (no marketing without consent)  
- For security and fraud prevention

## 3. AI Chatbot (Google Gemini) & Third Parties
Chatbot powered by Google Gemini API.  
- Inputs and outputs sent to Google servers for real-time processing  
- Recent history stored by us for context  
- Google's processing governed by their Privacy Policy & Gemini API Terms  

**Important Warning**: Do not share highly sensitive information (health, mental, financial) in chats – data is transmitted to Google. The App is not for medical/therapeutic use.

## 4. Third-Party Services

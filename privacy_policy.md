# Privacy Policy for Self Help App

**Last Updated: December 29, 2025**

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Self Help App" ("App"), operated by Tomer Noy ("Service Provider," "we," "us," "our"), collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
When you use the App, we may automatically collect:
- Device information (e.g., operating system, device type).
- Usage data (e.g., pages visited, time spent in the App, session duration).
- Approximate location (if enabled, for potential future features; currently not used for personalization).
- Internet Protocol (IP) address.

We do **not** collect precise geolocation data.

### User-Provided Information
To use certain features (e.g., account creation, chatbot), you may provide:
- Email address.
- Password.
- Display name.
- User ID (UID).
- Google Sign-In data (if using Google login).
- AI Chatbot conversation history (messages sent to/received from the chatbot).

Conversation history is stored temporarily on our secure servers solely to maintain context during your session and is deleted shortly after.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain App features (e.g., chatbot responses, exercises).
- To improve the App and analyze usage trends (anonymized and aggregated data only).
- To communicate important updates or notices (no marketing without consent).
- For security and fraud prevention.

We do **not** use your data for advertising, marketing promotions, or training AI models.

## 3. AI Chatbot and Third-Party Processing (Google Gemini)
The App's chatbot is powered by the Google Gemini API.
- Your chat inputs (prompts) and generated responses are sent to Google's servers for real-time processing.
- We do not store full conversation history long-term.
- Google's handling of this data is governed by their [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Gemini API Terms](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms).
- **Important**: Do not share highly sensitive personal information (e.g., health details, financial data) in chats, as it is transmitted to Google. The App is not intended for medical or therapeutic use.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App uses third-party services with their own privacy policies:
- Google Play Services / Firebase (for analytics, authentication): [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Google Gemini API: [Gemini Additional Terms](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms)

We may share anonymized, aggregated data with trusted providers for App improvement. We do not sell personal data.

## 5. Data Disclosure
We may disclose information:
- To comply with legal requirements.
- To protect rights, safety, or investigate fraud.
- With service providers bound by confidentiality.

## 6. Data Security
We use reasonable physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. However, no method is 100% secure.

## 7. Data Retention
- Temporary chat context: Deleted after session.
- Account data: Retained while your account is active.
- Upon request or account deletion: Deleted within a reasonable time.

Contact us to request deletion.

## 8. Children's Privacy
The App is for users 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or higher age where required). If discovered, we will delete it immediately.

## 9. Your Rights
- Access, correct, or delete your data: Contact us.
- Opt-out of collection: Uninstall the App.
- For EU/UK users: Additional rights under GDPR (contact us).

## 10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. Check regularly.
